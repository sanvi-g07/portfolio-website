The Worcester County Superintendent Association recognizes Superintendent Scholars every year from each of the 53 member districts for displaying exemplary academic excellence, leadership, and community involvement. This year, I was informed I have been selected as one of two Worcester County Superintendent Scholar Award recipients from my school district.

Student Council Awards

My Student Council operates on a point system. You have to participate in non-mandatory events to get points, and you need a certain number of points each quarter to stay on Student Council. At the end of the year, an award is given to the Top 10 Point Earners for being the most active members on council, and I am proud to say I have won this award my freshman, sophomore, and junior year.

Along with the Top 10 awards and an award for a council member of each grade, an award for the Student Council MVP is given by the president of Student Council. I was honored to be the recipient of the Student Council MVP award my sophomore year.
