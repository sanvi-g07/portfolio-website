About Me

Hi! I’m Sanvi, and I’m a senior at Westborough High School. I’ve lived in Westborough for the past 10 years now, but before that, I was basking in the California sun. The move to Massachusetts in the middle of winter wasn’t exactly pleasant, but experiencing my first snow day—getting to make a snow angel, have a snowball fight, and build a snowman (things I’d only ever seen on TV before)—won me over. I’ve made so many memories here and am excited to gain new experiences and create even more!

Although I love computer science, I also enjoy art, reading, and having movie nights with my friends. My favorite book is currently Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen. I read it for the first time in my English class junior year, and I absolutely fell in love with its witty, sarcastic humor and the character of Elizabeth Bennet. My favorite movie is Into the Spiderverse; I loved the story and found it visually stunning. I'm looking forward to the release of Beyond the Spiderverse!

Please look around my website to learn more about my different activities and projects! All the code for this website can be found on my GitHub.
