About Me

Hi! My name is Sanvi Gowda, and I am a senior at Westborough High School. I've lived in Westborough for the past 10 years now, but I used to live in California. I moved during winter - the weather adjustment was rough.

Although I love computer science, I also enjoy doodling, reading, and having movie nights with my friends. My favorite book currently is Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen. I read it for the first time in my English class junior year, and I absolutely fell in love with the witty, sarcastic writing and the character of Elizabeth Bennet. My favorite movie is Into the Spiderverse, since I loved the story and it was so visually stunning. I'm looking forward to the release of Beyond the Spiderverse!
